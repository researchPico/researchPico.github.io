How do we work?

PICO will strongly rely on collaboration among participants and researchers. As a participant, you will have the power and opportunity to be involved in the revolutionary research world. Your unique background will contribute to the development of new findings in our society. As well, if discussed with your researcher, there is a potential opportunity for you to earn money for your participation. As a researcher, you will have the chance to connect with various research participants that are eligible for your study. Considering that many research projects are on hold due to current pandemic restrictions, this is an opportunity for you to consider innovative ways to re-design your study and re-plan your research budget with the goal to promote research participation and to conduct your study.

What type of studies can I be involved in?

PICO wants to promote the creation of diverse research studies across different disciplines. We love creativity and we want to empower you throughout this process. Information about the study will be discussed between the participant and researcher after they match.

How much time do I have to dedicate for a research study?

Each study will create a unique experience for you. Information about the length and requirements of the study will be discussed between the participant and researcher after they match. Research compensation can be discussed between the participant and the researcher.

Can I get paid to participate in a research study?

Yes! Discussions about financial reimbursement should happen between research participants and researchers. PICO will help researchers save money on transportation, venue rental, poster printing, advertising, food and drinks and other expenses needed to recruit participants. This is an opportunity to reflect on research spendings and reinvest them in innovative methods that can have greater benefit.
